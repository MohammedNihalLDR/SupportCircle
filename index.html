About Us

SupportCircle is an innovative platform that connects donors with care homes and individuals in need. We're revolutionizing how communities support their most vulnerable members through technology, transparency, and heartfelt connections.

Our platform raises awareness about nearby care homes, encourages people to donate money, food, and essential resources, and builds a strong, sustainable community of support.

By bringing society together through both digital and physical initiatives, SupportCircle ensures consistent support, spreads hope, and makes it easy for everyone to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.

Our Story

Our Founding Story

During a university induction program, a group of students visited a local care home where many elderly and disabled people reside. What they discovered moved them deeply - severe shortages of money, food, medical supplies, and most importantly, community awareness and support.

Witnessing firsthand the challenges faced by these care homes, the students were inspired to create a sustainable solution. They realized that the problem wasn't a lack of compassion in society, but rather a disconnect between those who wanted to help and those who needed help.

This realization sparked the creation of SupportCircle, a platform designed to build bridges between care homes and donors, making donations easier, more transparent, and ultimately more impactful. What started as a university project has now grown into a movement that's transforming communities.

Vision & Mission

Our Mission

To empower communities by connecting donors and care homes through an intuitive, transparent platform that raises awareness of local needs, fosters active participation, and delivers timely support in the form of donations and essential resources to transform lives meaningfully and sustainably.

Our Vision

To inspire a world where every care home and individual in need is supported through a united, compassionate community, spreading hope, resources, and care, ensuring no one is overlooked and every act of kindness creates a lasting impact that reverberates through generations.
